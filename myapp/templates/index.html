Building Your Yoga Asana
"How do I build a sequence?" This is a question every yogi asks, whether you are perfecting your home practice or becoming an instructor-bulding a sequence is the most challenging part. So what do we do? Bring a notebook to class, write everything down and have to keep checking our list as we flow? No, that wont work now will it? I can help you build your flow where it becomes natural-like a dance. There are key elements we will cover in our sessions:
  • Your Style
  • Your Strengths
  • Class/Practice Vibe
  • Who are your students?
  • Outdoor vs Indoor Sequence
